Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our security packages

How long does installation take?

Installation time varies based on the package and property size. For Essential Security, installation typically takes 3-4 hours. Business Protection takes 6-8 hours, and Enterprise Security installations usually require 1-2 days.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes, you can upgrade your security package at any time. We offer flexible upgrade paths and will credit your original package cost toward your upgrade. Contact our support team to discuss upgrade options.

Do you offer monitoring services?

Yes, our Business Protection and Enterprise Security packages include 24/7 professional monitoring. Our monitoring center is UL-certified and staffed with trained security professionals who can dispatch emergency services when needed.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and offer financing options with 0% interest for qualified customers. We also accept payment through PayPal and other digital payment methods.

Is professional installation included?

Yes, all our packages include professional installation by certified technicians. We handle everything from system design to installation and configuration, ensuring optimal performance of your security system.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel within 30 days for a full refund. After 30 days, we offer pro-rated refunds based on unused service time. Monitoring services can be canceled with 30 days notice. Please review our terms for full details.
